# GitHub Discussions Categories

Create these GitHub Discussions categories for the public PunamIDE downloads and community repository.

## General

General PunamIDE discussion, announcements, and community conversation.

## Alpha Feedback

Feedback from alpha testers, including what worked well, what was confusing, and what needs improvement.

## Feature Requests

Suggestions for new PunamIDE features, workflow improvements, and product direction.

## Showcase

Share projects, workflows, screenshots, and examples of PunamIDE in use.

## Q&A

Questions about installing, using, troubleshooting, or understanding PunamIDE.
